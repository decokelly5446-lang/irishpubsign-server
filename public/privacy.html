Who We Are

IrishPubSign.com is operated by Digital Impact Agency, Dublin, Ireland. We sell personalised Irish pub sign prints shipped worldwide. You can contact us at hello@irishpubsign.com.

What Data We Collect

When you place an order we collect your name, delivery address, email address, and payment information. Payment details are processed securely by Stripe and are never stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to process and fulfil your order, send you order confirmation and shipping updates, and handle any customer service queries. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Cookies

We use essential cookies required to process payments via Stripe. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can accept or reject non-essential cookies via the banner on our homepage.

Third Parties

We share your delivery address with Gelato, our print and fulfilment partner, solely for the purpose of printing and shipping your order. Stripe processes all payments securely.

Data Retention

We retain order data for 7 years as required by Irish tax law. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@irishpubsign.com, subject to legal retention requirements.

Your Rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights contact us at hello@irishpubsign.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission at dataprotection.ie.

Contact

For any privacy queries email hello@irishpubsign.com.
